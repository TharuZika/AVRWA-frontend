Welcome to Automated Vehicle Recognition

The Vehicle Recognition Web Application is an innovative solution designed to streamline the process of identifying and categorizing vehicles from uploaded images. Leveraging cutting-edge technologies, the application will provide users with an intuitive and efficient tool to recognize various types of vehicles, such as cars, vans, buses, planes, etc. The system will utilize a user-friendly frontend developed in Angular or React, coupled with a robust Python-based backend for accurate and real-time vehicle recognition.
